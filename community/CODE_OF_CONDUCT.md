# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Autoconnecto community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Encouraged:**

- Sharing projects, learnings, and constructive feedback
- Helping others with clear, reproducible steps
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experience levels

**Not allowed:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Spam, unsolicited advertising, or repeated self-promotion without context
- Publishing others’ private information (tokens, passwords, tenant secrets)
- Impersonation or misleading claims about Autoconnecto or partners

## Scope

This applies in Discord, GitHub (this repo and related org repos), events, and any space representing the community.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to **support@autoconnecto.in** or **founder@autoconnecto.in**. We may warn, remove content, or ban repeat offenders. We aim to respond within a few business days.
